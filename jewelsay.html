Our Story

✶

Sayali Kulkarni, the visionary behind Jewel Say, embarked on this journey in December 2017. What started as an experiment on social media platforms like Instagram and Facebook quickly blossomed into a thriving venture. Sayali, with a rare inclination for quality, ensured that every piece shone brilliantly and was skin-friendly. Her relentless pursuit of excellence led to partnerships with skilled, homegrown manufacturers. Now, Jewel Say, both retailers and wholesalers, specializes in Oxidized and Gold Plated Kolhapuri Jewelry, a symbol of their unwavering commitment to quality and unique style expression.
